About Me

Hey! I'm Shaik Nouhid, a DevOps & Cloud Engineer based in Bengaluru, India. I'm deeply passionate about containers, cloud infrastructure, and the art of automating everything that can be automated.

My journey is rooted in Linux — where I learned that understanding the system at its core makes you a better engineer everywhere else. From there it was Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, and a growing obsession with storage architecture.

I believe great infrastructure is invisible — it just works, scales silently, and never wakes anyone up at 3am. That's what I'm building toward, one kubectl apply at a time.

Currently levelling up in advanced Kubernetes internals, AWS cloud services, and exploring distributed storage systems — always building, always learning.
